Game

Get to School is a game where you get to school. Nothing else. Totally nothing else. Hope you survive! To play, click the link at the top labeled "game". Dummy.

Version 1.0

Version 1.0 of GTS2 is the base of the game, with two maps, Outside of Your House, School, and Inside House. Not much you can do besides check the time to see if it is your day to go, and get some gold from a chest. Check full patch notes for more.
